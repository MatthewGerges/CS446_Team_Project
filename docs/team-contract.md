## Communication

- Primary communication will occur through **Discord**.
- Important decisions and updates will be summarized in ./docs/meetings.md.
- GitHub Issues and Pull Requests will be used to track work and discussion related to the codebase.

---

## Meetings

- The team will meet **once per week** at **Tuesday at 3PM**, with additional meetings scheduled as needed.
- Meetings will have a clear agenda prepared ahead of time.
- All members are expected to attend meetings or notify the team in advance if they are unable to attend.
- Someone will be designated to take notes for each meeting, and notes will be stored in the [Meeting minutes](./docs/meetings.md) directory.

### Meeting Expectations

- Members will arrive prepared and engaged.
- Decisions may be made even if not all members are present.
- Everyone will have the opportunity to speak, and active listening is expected.

---

## Work Expectations

- All members are expected to contribute consistently throughout the term.
- Work should be completed on time or communicated early if delays are expected.
- Code contributions should follow agreed-upon standards and be reviewed via pull requests.
- Large decisions should be discussed with the team before implementation.

---

## Conflict Resolution

Conflict is expected and can be constructive when handled properly.

- The team will prioritize **collaboration, compromise, and open discussion**.
- Conflicts should be addressed early and respectfully.
- If a conflict cannot be resolved internally, the team will seek guidance from course staff.
- Team members will treat conflicts as disagreements between allies, not opponents.

---

## Accountability

- If a team member is not meeting expectations, the issue should be raised respectfully with the individual.
- If problems persist, the team lead will facilitate a group discussion.
- As a last resort, course staff may be consulted.
